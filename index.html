About Us

At Dutt Exports and Imports, we are passionate about connecting farms to global markets with integrity, reliability, and quality. Based in India, we specialize in the export of fresh fruits, vegetables, spices, and agricultural commodities that meet international standards. With a strong network of trusted farmers and suppliers, we ensure that every product is handpicked, carefully sorted, and packaged to preserve its freshness and nutritional value. What sets us apart is our commitment to quality assurance, on-time delivery, and customized packaging solutions to meet the needs of clients worldwide. Whether you are an importer, wholesaler, retailer, or food processor, we provide products that add value to your business.

Elephant Yam (Suran)

Elephant Foot Yam (Suran) is a starchy tropical tuber prized for its neutral, earthy flavor and excellent texture in curries, fries, chips, pickles, and ready-to-cook cubes

Mangoes

Mango is a tropical stone fruit celebrated for its vibrant sweetness, aromatic flavor, and juicy pulp, making it a global favorite for fresh consumption, juices, desserts, jams, and value-added processed products.

Green Chilli (Mirchi)

Chilli is a versatile spice crop valued for its pungency, color, and aroma, widely used in curries, sauces, pickles, powders, and seasoning blends across cuisines worldwide.
